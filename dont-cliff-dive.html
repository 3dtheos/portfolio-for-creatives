Don't Cliff Dive

This project was inspired by the time I visited Lynn Canyon Valley during New Year's Day, 2024. I saw signs around the park warning people not to cliff dive, and it sparked my curiosity about the phenomenon. Later, I learned that it was a thrill-seeking pastime for young people in Vancouver, and so I was inspired to make a short PSA type animation meant to be posted by social media teams of people running parks like Lynn Canyon Valley, which has cliffs overlooking rivers that may attract cliff divers.

For the animation I employed a scratchy, cartoony aesthetic showcasing a cliff diver eagerly jumping down to a body of for thrills, but unfortunately getting injured on the rocks and getting swept away by the current. With the help of my instructor’s feedback the tagline “not all dangers are visible”, warning cliff divers that even though cliff diving looks fun and safe, it may not actually be.
