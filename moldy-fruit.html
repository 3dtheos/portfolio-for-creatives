Moldy Fruit

An educational, short motion graphics animation I did for school. I animated and edited the photos used in this, and I modeled, rigged, and rendered the claymation stick figure. I also wrote the text you see in the video!

SOURCES:

Typeface: Old Newspaper Types by Manfred Klein.

Sound effects: AEJUICE Free Plugins.

Music: Frog Legs Rag by James Scott, 1906 Piano Roll, slowed & reverb effect added by me.
