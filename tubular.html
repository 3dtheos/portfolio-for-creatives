Tubular

Tubular is my first variable font. An ambitious project, it intends to be a font with individually rotating glyphs and variable weights. Each glyph was drawn and vectorized by hand, to give the impression of a really organic, twisting material.

You may download the font on my Ko-Fi page!Thank you for looking!
